Meal Recipe App

Purpose : a food recipe app that helps users discover meals by area or ingredients, view detailed recipes, save their favourites and also random meal .


Core Features:
Search meals by name
filter meals by area
click a mean to see full recipe details
display ingredients , instructions and yt video maybe


Nice to have (chatgpt)
save fav meals (local)
filter/search by ingredient or category
surprise me (random meal)
dark mode or theme switcher
